CONTOURS

 

Introduction

SWOOD CAM allows the treatment of the various contours that may be present on a part. (internal and external contours). In order to carry out the machining of these contours, the following operations are available according to the type of selection:

  1. Contour
  2. Staydown Contour
  3. Outline Open Pocket
  4. Partial contouring
  5. Move Tool Center
  6. Helical Interpolation
  7. Interpolate C Axis
  8. Outline Contour
  9. Resumed Angle
  10. Corner Notching
  11. Resumed Corner After Saw
  12. Chamfer
  13. 5-axis Edge Follow
  14. Saw
  15. Waste Cutting

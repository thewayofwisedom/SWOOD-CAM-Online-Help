WOODWOP POCKET MILLING

 

Introduction

WOODWOP Pocket milling is an operation to process blind geometries in a part. Contrary to the operation Pocket Milling, this operation does not export a trajectory but allows to export in WOODWOP a pocket macro with the parameters indicated in SWOOD CAM.
This operation is available for any free form, except for pockets with islands.

 

Selection

  1. By selecting the bottom of a pocket, it is possible to machine a pocket by clicking  on Milling .
  2. By selecting a sketch  in the SOLIDWORKS Feature manager tree and clicking  on Milling .
  3. By selecting a closed outline from the SolidWorks graphical interface and clicking  on Milling .
  4. By selecting a closed loop (edges) from the SolidWorks GUI and clicking  on Milling .

Once the tool and the WOODWOP Pocket milling have been selected, the operation configuration window will appear on the screen.

 

 

Interface

The operation dialogue in SWOOD CAM displays the same parameters available in WOODWOP.

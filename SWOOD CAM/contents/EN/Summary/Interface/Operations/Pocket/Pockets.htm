POCKETS

Introduction

SWOOD CAM allows to process the various pockets that may be present on a part. (Open, closed, non-open or open pockets). In order to machine these pockets, the following operations are available according to the type of selection:

  1. Pocket milling
  2. Bottom pocket finishing
  3. Walls of pocket finishing
  4. Mortising
  5. Resumed pocket angle
  6. Resume angle using radius compensation
  7. Angle Finishing
  8. Cut-groove
  9. WOODWOP Pocket milling
  10. Machine Pocket milling

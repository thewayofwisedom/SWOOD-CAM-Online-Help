SURFACES

Introduction

SWOOD CAM allows the treatment of various surfaces that may be present on a part. In order to carry out the machining of these surfaces, the following operations are available according to the type of selection:

  1. Non-flat surfaces
    1. Roughing
    2. Finishing
  2. Flat surfaces
    1. Pocket Milling
    2. Surface
  3. Other types of surfaces
    1. Clamex
    2. Clamex With Milling Tool

EDIT SELECTION: SURFACE

It is possible to edit the selection of the " Surface" machining by  right-clicking on the machining.

 

INTERFACE

The edit selection window is composed of several parameters.

 

PARAMETERS

The 4 parameters of the surface selection editing window are used to represent the 3D surface as a mesh of triangles.

These parameters are represented using cursors, the more a cursor is positioned to the right, the more precise the triangulation will be but the longer the calculation time will be.

  • "Surface Deviation": Maximum distance between the surface of the model and the surface of a triangle. Min. value 1mm, max. value 0.01 mm.

 

  • "Edge Deviation": Maximum distance between the surface of the model and the edge of a triangle. Min. value 1mm, max. value 0.01 mm.

 

  • "Angle / Surface": Maximum angle of deviation between the model surface and the surface of a triangle.

 

  • "Angle / Edge": Maximum angle of deviation between the surface of the model and the edge of a triangle.

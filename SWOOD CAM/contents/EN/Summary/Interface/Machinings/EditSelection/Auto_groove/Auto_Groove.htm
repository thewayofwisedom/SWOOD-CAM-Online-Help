EDIT SELECTION: AUTO GROOVE

It is possible to edit the selection of the " Auto groove" machining by  right-clicking on the machining.

INTERFACE

The edit selection window is composed of several parameters.

 

PARAMETERS

Body selection

It is possible to select which bodies will be impacted by the machining:

  • "All bodies": All the bodies of the model will be impacted by the machining.
  • "Selected bodies": Only the bodies selected in the selection box will be impacted by the machining.

 

Dimensional limits

It is possible to define dimensional limits beyond which the grooves will not be included in the machining.

  • "Maximum width": Width beyond which the groove will not be considered as a groove, because it is too long to machine as such.
  • "Minimum length": Length below which the groove will not be considered as a groove, because it's no longer relevant to machine as such.

 

Exclusions

It is possible to exclude certain grooves from the selection according to certain criteria.

  • "Remove groove through all": Does not take into account grooves that cut through the entire thickness of the part.

  • "Remove closed grooves": Does not take into account grooves that don't have at least one open side.

  • "Remove grooves with only one opened side": Does not take into account grooves that have only one open side.

  • "Remove grooves with both sides opened": Does not take into account grooves that have two open sides.

  • "Remove grooves with both sides opened along:" "X" or "Y": Does not take into account grooves that have two open sides, along the X or Y axis.

X                                                                  Y

              

  • "Remove grooves with cylindrical floor": Does not take into account grooves that have a bottom made of cylindrical faces.

  • "Remove non rectangle grooves": Does not take into account grooves that have a shape that is not a rectangle.

  • "Remove direction:": Does not take into account all grooves that have the following direction:

X                                                    Y                                                   Other

      

 

Other parameters

  • "Groove based on flat floor": Defines the groove from the flat part of the bottom of the groove.

  • "Extend the height to the maximum of the part": Defines the top of the groove from the highest height of the part.

  • "Look for:" "Z-" or "Z+": Defines the groove from the top or top and bottom geometries of the part.

 Machining are always done from the top of the part.

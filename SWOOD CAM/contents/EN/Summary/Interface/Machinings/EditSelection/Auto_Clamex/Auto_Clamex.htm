EDIT SELECTION: AUTO CLAMEX

It is possible to edit the selection of the " Auto Clamex" machining by  right-clicking on the machining.

 

INTERFACE

The edit selection window is composed of several parameters.

 

PARAMETERS

Body selection

It is possible to select which bodies will be impacted by the machining:

  • "All bodies": All the bodies of the model will be impacted by the machining.
  • "Selected bodies": Only the bodies selected in the selection box will be impacted by the machining.

 

Dimensional limits

It is possible to define dimensional limits beyond which the clamex will not be included in the machining.

  • "Minimum Thickness": Thickness below which the geometry will not be considered as a clamex, as it is too thin to machine.
  • "Maximum Thickness": Thickness above which the geometry will not be considered as a clamex, because it is too thick to machine.
  • "Minimum Radius": Radius below which the geometry will not be considered as a clamex, because it is too far from the tool radius.
  • "Maximum Radius": Radius above which the geometry will not be considered as a clamex, because it is too far from the tool radius.

 

Exclusions

It is possible to exclude certain clamex from the selection according to certain criteria.

  • "Remove OP0": Does not take into account clamex machinable in OP0 (horizontal clamex).

  • "Remove Face 1": Does not take into account clamex machinable on Face 1 (vertical clamex in X direction).

  • "Remove Face 2": Does not take into account clamex machinable on Face 2 (vertical clamex in Y direction).

  • "Remove clamex in tilted face": Does not take into account inclined clamex.

EDIT SELECTION: AUTO POCKET

It is possible to edit the selection of the " Auto pocket" machining by right-clicking on the machining.

 

INTERFACE

The edit selection window is composed of several parameters.

 

PARAMETERS

Body selection

It is possible to select which bodies will be impacted by the machining:

  • "All bodies": All the bodies of the model will be impacted by the machining.
  • "Selected bodies": Only the bodies selected in the selection box will be impacted by the machining.

 

Extension

It is possible to extend the dimensions of the pockets according to the following parameter:

  • "Extend open pockets width to the limits of the part": Defines the dimensions of the open pockets according to the dimensions of the part.

 

Exclusions

It is possible to exclude certain pockets from the selection according to certain criteria.

  • "Remove rectangle pockets": Does not take into account rectangular pockets.

 

  • "Remove oblong pockets": Does not take into account oblong shaped pockets.

 

  • "Remove rounded rectangle pockets": Does not take into account rectangular pockets with rounded corners.

 

  • "Remove rounded pockets": Does not take into account circular pockets.

 

  • "Remove other pockets": Does not take into account pockets of undefined or random shapes.

 

  • "Pocket profil based on bottom face if Origine is not OP0": Does not take into account the pockets whose bottom is open on an origin different from OP0 (indexed origin).

EDIT SELECTION: AUTO FLAT FACE

It is possible to edit the selection of the " Auto flat face" machining by  right-clicking on the machining.

 

INTERFACE

The edit selection window is composed of several parameters.

 

PARAMETERS

Body selection

It is possible to select which bodies will be impacted by the machining:

  • "All bodies": All the bodies of the model will be impacted by the machining.
  • "Selected bodies": Only the bodies selected in the selection box will be impacted by the machining.

 

Exclusions

It is possible to exclude certain sawing from the selection according to certain criteria.

  • "Remove vertical saw plane": Does not include sawing from vertical planes except for faces 1, 2, 3 and 4.

 

  • "Remove saw plane Z+ oriented": Does not take into account inclined sawing on the upper side of the part.

 

  • "Remove saw plane Z- oriented": Does not take into account inclined sawing on the underside of the part.

 

  • "Remove saw plane on face 1": Does not take into account face 1 (front face) as sawing.

 

  • "Remove saw plane on face 2": Does not take into account face 2 (right face) as sawing.

 

  • "Remove saw plane on face 3": Does not take into account face 3 (back face) as sawing.

 

  • "Remove saw plane on face 4": Does not take into account face 4 (left face) as sawing.

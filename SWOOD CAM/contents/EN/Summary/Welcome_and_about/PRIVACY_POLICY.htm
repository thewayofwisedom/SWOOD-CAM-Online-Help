3. PERSONAL DATA CONCERNING CHILDREN

EFICAD products and services are not designed to be marketed to children who are considered as minor under the applicable data protection law and EFICAD does not knowingly collect or store Personal Data from children under such age. For children whose usual place of residence is in the European Union, Lichtenstein, Norway or Iceland, the default age at which a person is no longer considered a child is 16 subject to local law to adjust that limit between 13 and 16.

Certain EFICAD computer software application may be appropriate for use by children at school, however, EFICAD does not knowingly collect or store Personal Data from such children for such use.

If parents or guardians become aware that their child has provided EFICAD with Personal Data without their consent or without a teacher’s or school administrator’s consent, they can ask EFICAD to remove such Personal Data and terminate the child’s account by sending an email to the EFICAD Data Protection Officer: privacy@eficad.com.

If EFICAD becomes aware that it has collected Personal Data from a child below the age of consent under local data protection laws without consent from a parent or guardian, EFICAD will take steps to seek the parent’s or guardian’s consent for that processing or to remove such Personal Data and terminate the child’s account.

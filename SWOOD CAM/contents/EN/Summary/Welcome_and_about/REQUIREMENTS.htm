REQUIREMENTS

 

To get the best compatibility between SWOOD and SOLIDWORKS, some SOLIDWORKS options must be verified after the cretion of the SWOOD User directory.

 

 

Dynamic Highlight

To use the SWOOD Design drag and drop functions, the "dynamic graphical view Highlight" must be activated in the SOLIDWORKS options. For this, it is necessary to verify that the option is checked

  • Tools →   Options →   Viewing/Selection →   Highlight Dynamic Graphics View.
